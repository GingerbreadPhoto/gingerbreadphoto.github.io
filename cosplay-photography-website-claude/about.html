About

The person behind the lens

Part photographer, part fan — all about making your character look just the way they do in your head.

My story

Gingerbread Photography, but no cookies!

My name it Or! I am a cosplay photographer based in Israel. I have started to do cosplay phoography in March of 2018. When I showed interest in photography, picking up on my dads hobby, it was clear to me that the passion for it passed in the family. When I decided to pick up a camera, a friend of mine in the cosplay community came to me and asked me if I would like to try, and ever Cosplay Photography and now, 8 years later, I can say I am a professional!

My style of photography is focused on atmospheric, cinmeatic and narrative driven shots, I love to bring the characters I shoot to life and immerse them in the environment.

How I work

The approach

  1. Plan the story

    Before the shoot we talk character, key poses, and mood — a few reference stills from the source material go a long way.

  2. Find the light

    Location and lighting choices come from the character's world: gothic staircases, forest ruins, neon streets — whatever fits.

  3. Shoot in character

    I direct poses and expressions the whole way — you never have to guess what to do with your hands.

  4. Cinematic finish

    Careful color grading and retouching that stays faithful to your craftsmanship — enhancing the costume, never repainting it.
